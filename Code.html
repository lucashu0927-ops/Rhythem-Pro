How To Play

  1. Press D F J K to hit the 4 lanes.
  2. Wait for the falling note to touch the glowing line.
  3. Only Perfect removes the note.
  4. Good and Bad do not remove the note.
  5. Miss removes the note and subtracts 500 points.
